Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This application connects to your social media accounts (such as TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) with your explicit authorization. We access only the permissions you grant, including the ability to publish content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your social media account access solely to schedule and publish content that you create and approve. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data or social media data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account credentials and access tokens are stored securely on our private servers. We do not store your social media passwords. Authentication is handled through each platform's official OAuth process.

4. Data Retention

You may disconnect your social media accounts at any time. Upon disconnection, your access tokens are deleted from our system. Published content remains on the respective social media platforms.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with third-party social media platforms. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Contact
